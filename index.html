ABOUT SESoc

SESoc (Software Engineering Society) at The Open University of Sri Lanka is a dynamic community dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and professional growth among software engineering students.
It provides opportunities for hands-on learning, networking, and skill development through workshops, events, and industry collaborations, empowering students to excel in the tech field.
